# Another Day

## Why am I making this game?
Because in real life, everyday seems repetative. I want everyday to feel exciting, like waking up and opening today's mystery box. I love exploring, experimenting and oh not every game has to be about saving the world !!! 
I want to remind people that small achivements matter.
I want to create the kind of game I'd play after a tiring day.

## What should the player feel every morning?
Umm...
Closing my eyes.
The loading screen fades.
Music starts.
The character wakes up.
What's in your head?
Maybe:
"Oooo... what's happening today?"
or
"I have absolutely no idea what's coming."
or
"Today's gonna be fun."
or
"Something weird is definitely about to happen."
or
"Whatever happens, I'll handle it."
or
"Let's make today count."
My game isn't about fear.
It's about curiosity.

## What should the player feel before sleeping?
This is my favorite question.
What should happen when the player presses Sleep?
Maybe...
"I actually did something today."
"I helped someone."
"I laughed."
"I failed... but tomorrow is another day."
"I can't wait to wake up tomorrow."
"I wonder where I'll be next."
"I collected another memory."
Notice...
The game isn't rewarding players only with points.
It's rewarding them with closure.
