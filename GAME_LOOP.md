# Game Loop

## 1. Wake Up

The player wakes up.

---

## 2. Morning Chaos

A random event happens.

Examples:
- Ceiling leak
- Cat steals sock
- Wrong parcel
- Dream
- No electricity

The player deals with it.

---

## 3. Discover Today's Goal

The player learns today's main objective.

Examples:
- Attend interview
- Help a stranger
- Find lost item
- Reach railway station
- Deliver package

---

## 4. Prepare

Player can

- eat
- drink coffee
- pack bag
- check weather
- choose outfit (optional)

---

## 5. Explore

Leave the house.

Walk around.

Travel.

Meet NPCs.

Discover random events.

---

## 6. Complete Today's Goal

The player either:

- succeeds
- fails
- ignores it

Each outcome changes something.

---

## 7. Evening

Return home.

Relax.

Read journal.

Look at today's photos.

Decorate room.

---

## 8. Sleep

Player decides to sleep.

The day comes to an end.

The journal records what happened.

The player keeps memories, photos, items and relationships.

Tomorrow begins.
